Rahul Adhikari is an experienced Senior Security Engineer specializing in Android Threat Intelligence and Reverse Engineering, formerly with the Google Operations Center (GOC). With over eight years of experience in mobile security, malware analysis, and threat intelligence, he focuses on dissecting sophisticated mobile malware targeting native libraries, analyzing obfuscated Android applications, and documenting end-to-end attack chains.

## Technical Specialties & Research Areas

* **Android Native Reverse Engineering & Unpacking:** Expert in static and dynamic analysis of complex Android native libraries (C/C++/Rust) and custom virtual machines. Expert in decompiling and deobfuscating native binaries using Ghidra, IDA Pro, and Radare2.
* **Dynamic Instrumentation & Sandbox Evasion:** Proficient in developing custom Frida and Xposed hooks to bypass anti-debugging, root detection, and SSL pinning mechanisms.
* **Threat Intelligence & Attribution:** Specializes in mapping Command and Control (C2) infrastructure, decrypting custom network protocols, creating robust YARA/Snort signatures, and attributing campaigns to threat actors or APT groups via code reuse and behavioral profiling.
